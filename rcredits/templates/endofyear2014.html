Holy cow, we've done it.

Lots of people doubted we could issue rCredits and have almost the same effect as issuing US Dollars. But we proved it! On December 15th we successfully completed our year-long pilot of rCredits in Greenfield, MA. All of the important features are finally in place, and running beautifully, and everyone is better off.


Here's a snapshot of the rCredits system as it is today, before we expand to more participants and additional communities:

Participants100 active members
14 companies
Transactionsabout 10 a day
$186,000 to date
rCredits issued to date$26,000 to participants, as incentive rewards
$55,000 for the community (not yet spent)
Credit card fees savedover $1,800

We're ready to grow.

Already, groups in 6 states are asking for our help to launch rCredits in their communities. A second pilot in Ann Arbor, MI, is nearly ready to begin. When we grow to 10,000 members in each of 10 communities, change the thousands to millions in the figures above.

More importantly, though, we are ready to begin the Democracy Phase of rCredits. Together we have the power to fund our common good. What do we want to do with this power? What do we value as a community? How can we use this power to make our community sustainable, provide for everyone's basic needs, and help other communities do the same? Our members' celebration on January 31st will begin that discussion.
